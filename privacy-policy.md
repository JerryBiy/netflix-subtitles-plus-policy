Privacy Policy for Netflix Subtitles+

Netflix Subtitles+ does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

Data Collection

The extension does not collect personally identifiable information, browsing history, or analytics data. It does not track users, display ads, or communicate with external servers.

Website Content

Netflix Subtitles+ interacts with website content on netflix.com only to display a subtitle overlay synchronized with video playback. All processing occurs locally in the user’s browser.

User-Provided Data

Any subtitle files (.srt or .vtt) loaded by the user are processed locally and are never uploaded, stored remotely, or shared with third parties.

Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s local storage to save user preferences such as subtitle timing offset, font size, position, and appearance settings. These settings remain on the user’s device and are not transmitted.

Third Parties

Netflix Subtitles+ does not share data with third parties and does not include third-party analytics or tracking libraries.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the extension’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected at this URL.
